The Official Presidential Campaign Website of John Fenley

Goals for a benevolent government:

  1. Eliminate suffering as far as possible.
  2. Increase abundance, sustainably, for the long term.
  3. Enjoy life.

Guiding Principles:

  1. Preserve personal freedom.
  2. Use technology effectively to advance civilization.
  3. Use the principles of open source to find and solve problems, and guide the operations of the government, quickly and effectively.
  4. Use prediction and data analysis to respond proactively to trends.

I believe that we, as a society, can solve any problem that we are truly determined to solve.
